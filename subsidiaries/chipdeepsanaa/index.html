About ChipDeepSanaa™

Africa's First AI Silicon Startup

ChipDeepSanaa™ — meaning "deep craftsmanship" in Swahili — was born from the conviction that the continent that birthed humanity should also birth the hardware powering its AI future.

Founded under YEGOZCORP.™ by Kiprop Yego, we are building full-stack AI silicon capability in East Africa: from architecture and RTL design to physical implementation and packaging.
