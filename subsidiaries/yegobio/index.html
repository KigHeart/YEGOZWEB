About YEGOBIO™

Biotechnology Rooted in Africa

YEGOBIO™ was founded with a clear mandate: Africa carries the highest global disease burden, yet receives a fraction of global biotech investment. We are changing that.

By combining cutting-edge molecular biology with AI-native drug discovery and the world's most genetically diverse genomics datasets, we are building therapeutics that work for everyone — starting with those left behind.
