Our Culture

Four Pillars of
the PotGodz World

We don't make products. We make positions. Every piece, every event, every collaboration is a statement about who we are and where we're going.

Street Intelligence

The convergence of street culture and intellectual depth. Wearing ideas, not just fabric. Dressing like you've read the footnotes.

African Futurism

Rooted in Nairobi, imagining futures that centre Africa not as a development story but as a design story — generative, not reactive.

Anti-Disposability

Radical Collaboration

We collaborate with artists, scientists, engineers, and rebels. The best work happens at the edges of categories, not inside them.

About PotGodz™

Born in Nairobi.
Built for Everywhere.

PotGodz™ began as an expression — a name Kiprop Yego scrawled in a notebook alongside quantum equations and chip architectures. It became a brand because it had to: the world needs proof that deep tech and street culture can occupy the same mind.

As a YEGOZCORP.™ subsidiary, PotGodz™ is the cultural arm of the enterprise — the part that reminds everyone that behind all the technology, there is a person with a voice, a vision, and a wardrobe.
