Our research is organised around the six pillars required to deliver real-world quantum utility.

Quantum Error Correction

Surface codes, color codes, and topological qubits to achieve fault-tolerant quantum computation at scale.

QEC · Fault Tolerance

Quantum Algorithms

Variational quantum eigensolvers, QAOA, and HHL algorithm implementations for chemistry, logistics, and finance.

VQE · QAOA · HHL

Seamless orchestration layers enabling quantum co-processors to offload specific subroutines from classical HPC clusters.

QML · Kernels

PQC · QKD

Our Platform

The ASISIAN Stack

A full-stack quantum computing platform: from qubit fabrication and control electronics to cloud-accessible quantum APIs and algorithm libraries.

Hardware Layer

Superconducting transmon qubits operating at 15mK with custom cryo-CMOS control electronics. Targeting 1000 physical qubits by 2027.

Software Stack

Open-source quantum SDK with Python bindings, quantum circuit compiler, noise-aware transpiler, and REST API for cloud quantum access.

Algorithm Library

Curated collection of quantum algorithms optimised for near-term noisy devices (NISQ) across chemistry, optimisation, and ML domains.

Cloud Access

Quantum-as-a-Service platform providing secure, authenticated access to ASISIAN quantum hardware for research and enterprise partners.
